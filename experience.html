Experience Data Dream

Here is the link to my Unity project.

City

This is the first layer of Data Dream, and users are invited to find clues on buildings that change texture. The three clues added together are "Find the Cloud." Stepping into the red destination bubble teleports the user into the next layer, the Categorization Garden.

Categorization Garden

The skybox changes every layer to create a sense of time passing. The background music also matches the ambience of every terrain. In the Categorization Garden, all the flowers look at the user. They are sorted by color, and some have data textures. Stepping into the transparent destination bubble teleports the user into the next layer, the Histogram Terrain.

Histogram Terrain

Here, the user enters a mountainous region, which echoes histograms. The user can explore the environment before stepping into the transparent destination bubble that teleports the user to the next layer, the Neural Net Forest.

Neural Net Forest

All of the trees here take the form of data nodes and networks, and they spin around the user. Many of the textures on the trees are patterned with technology, which infuses the environment as familiar and unfamiliar.

Cloud

You have reached your destination! This is the Cloud that we all know of that stores our data, yet it is an ambiguous concept to many. I visualized this into a space that the user can explore and feel. The red destination bubble will take the viewer back to the City.
